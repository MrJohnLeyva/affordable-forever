The first rally. The goal from day one was simple: that everyone in New York would know what was happening at 63 Tiffany Place. Seventy families, thirty years, and a tax credit about to run out.

It worked. The press came. The electeds came. And the landlord, who had ignored us for years, suddenly hired a law firm — which is how you know a rally landed.
