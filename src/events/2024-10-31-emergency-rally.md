title: "Save 63 Tiffany — bring the landlord back to the table"

location: 63 Tiffany Place, Brooklyn

Our landlord cut off negotiations. We had found a nonprofit preservation buyer, ready to make an offer. So we rallied outside our own front door and demanded he come back.

Our elected officials sent him a letter the day before. Comptroller Brad Lander, Public Advocate Jumaane Williams, Senator Zellnor Myrie (the Senate sponsor of TOPA), Assemblymember Marcela Mitaynes (the Assembly sponsor), Assemblymember Jo Anne Simon, Borough President Antonio Reynoso, Council Members Shahana Hanif and Alexa Avilés — all of them stood on that sidewalk with us.

Tenants waved signs reading **TOPA and COPA**. That is what a building looks like when it decides not to disappear quietly.
